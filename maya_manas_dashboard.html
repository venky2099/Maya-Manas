Maya-Manas Oscillatory Thalamo-Cortical Gating for Class-Incremental Learning in Affective SNNs

Manas (मनस्) — the oscillating, doubting, sensory-receiving mind — gates the fc1 LIF layer with a half-cycle cosine threshold descent, tracing the Vikalpa-to-Sankalpa transition within each forward pass. Only signals salient enough to spike at high threshold earn amplified Vairagya protection.

In plain terms: the network starts each moment with its eyes barely open — only the strongest signals get through first. Then it gradually opens up. What gets through early earns long-term protection. What sneaks in later does not.
